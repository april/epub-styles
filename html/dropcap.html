Dropcap

This page demonstrates the use of drop caps, also known as an initial. In a written or published work, an initial is a letter at the beginning of a word, a chapter, or a paragraph that is larger than the rest of the text. The word is derived from the Latin “initiālis,” which means of the beginning.
