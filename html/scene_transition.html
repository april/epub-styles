Scene Transition

This page demonstrates some of the ways you can divide a scene.

The first, and probably the most common way, is a happy little horizontal line. Ideally you would use a three-em dash, aka &#x2E3B;, but most fonts don’t support that character. Instead, we use a <hr> that is 10% of the page width.

—

Of course, that doesn't mean you can’t get fancy with things. If you’re an up-and-coming publisher who wants to make a sp—lash, why not create your very own custom divider set as the background image to a <div> instead?

Note that the Kindle doesn't support CSS color mode selection, so you have to get extremely hacky by making your separator into a font (see svg2font.py) and then using an em dash as your text.
