Text Messages

Text messages are a surprisingly common part of modern fiction, and have dozens of ways to format them. And yet, most of them are entirely unreadable.

This style features a bunch of unique features, including:

  • optional sender and recipient names
  • consecutive messages from the same person are closer together than messages from alternating people
  • full color on devices that can support it
  • messages that won't render partially on one page and partially on another
